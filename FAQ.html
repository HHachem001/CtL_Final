FAQ - Basic Income Tax Course Assistant

General Questions
What is this assistant's purpose?
The assistant is designed to enhance the learning experience in income tax by providing interactive and educational tools. It aims to facilitate a deeper understanding of complex tax concepts through user-friendly features.
Who can benefit from this assistant?
This assistant is beneficial for both students and individuals who want to learn or improve their knowledge of income tax. Whether you're a student studying tax law or an individual looking to better understand your own tax situation, this assistant can be a valuable resource.
Feature-Specific Questions
How do I interact with the IRC Chatbot?
You can interact with the IRC Chatbot by choosing between the A.I. ChatBot and Basic RegEx Chatbot. These chatbots provide a platform for you to ask tax-related questions and receive informative responses based on artificial intelligence or regular expression algorithms.
What can I expect in Quizzes and Exams?
Quizzes and exams are designed to test and reinforce your understanding of income tax concepts. They include a variety of questions that cover different aspects of tax law, helping you assess your knowledge and identify areas for improvement.
How does Course Notes Search function?
The Course Notes Search function allows you to efficiently retrieve specific course notes. You can use either the Search Engine or A.I. Search to find relevant information quickly, making your study process more streamlined and effective.
What are the other features available?
In addition to the chatbots and course notes search, you can access the IRC Code Extractor, view the course outline, and use the gains calculator for practical learning. These features provide a comprehensive toolkit to enhance your understanding and application of income tax concepts.
Technical Support and Current Known Issues
Why is the page slow or unresponsive?
The page may be slow or unresponsive due to the implementation of the A.I. library, which runs on your device. If the page freezes, it is likely generating a response. Patience is advised, or you can clear your browser's cache to ensure smooth interactions with the assistant.
Why is the A.I. Course Notes Search tool not functioning right?
The A.I. Course Notes Search tool uses two A.I. models to answer your questions. It uses the smaller, more limited language model to find your answer. If the confidence score is below a certain threshold, it hands off the question to the more complex AI model, which gives an answer. Currently, there is an issue where the questions gets "stuck" in the basic A.I. model is it fails to hand it off to the complex AI model. A feature is being tested that will enable you to override the basic AI model and force the handoff to the more complex AI.
